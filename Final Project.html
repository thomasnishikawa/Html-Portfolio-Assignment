A Peace of Mind

I wanted to make a 3d scene with multiple characters. Each character utilizes different art styles and shaders implying a personality and relationship. Despite how vastly different these characters are, they have an organized chaos to them. With the inclusion of myself or a representation of me this made me reframe how I saw the project. These aren't just random characters bothering each other. These characters each have a piece of myself within them. My experiences, my aesthetics, my skills, even the others around me and how I see them. In a way this final project is a self portrait representing many individual facets of myself.
