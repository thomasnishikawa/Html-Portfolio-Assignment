Discord Friends Slander MEME

This is a video I actually wanted to make for a few months now and this assignment was the perfect opportunity to do so. This is biased on the Slander MEME that was popular in 2022 where people would make fun of things within a topic with an assortment of clips. I wanted to make a video like this about my friends from Discord past and present.

There are many jokes and topics that are divorced from context and wouldn't make any sense to anyone outside of the server. However, what I hope people can gleam from this is the double-sided nature of online relationships. Where people can form genuine relationships and long-term friendships it can also expose you to toxic topics and people.
